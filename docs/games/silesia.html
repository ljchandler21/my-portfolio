Silesia

A first-person narrative exploration that tugs on the heartstrings.

When a letter from your recently deceased grandfather beckons you back to your family home in Silesia, you aren't sure what to expect. By exploring the space and the relics in it, learn the complicated past of a man you never fully knew. This is a story about hardship, tenacity, soul, and the people of Poland.


This game was made for the Dialogue of Civilizations: Poland in Pixels program. It was developed over a span of four weeks as a joint effort between students of the Polish-Japanese Academy of Information Technology in Warsaw and Northeastern University in Boston. Please note that Silesia is intended to be a short and polished experience, with an expected playtime of 5-10 minutes.


[Currently the quit to main menu button is broken and it is recommended to restart the game after each playthrough]


The browser build has lower resolution of textures, but offers the same experience. It is not a demo version. Playing with sound on is strongly recommended. You can use "P" to pause if you are playing the web version.
